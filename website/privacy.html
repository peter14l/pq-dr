Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22 June 2026  ·  Last Updated: 22 June 2026

TL;DR: We collect only what's necessary to process your payment and deliver your license. We do not sell your data. The PQ-Aura SDK itself does not collect any data from your users.

1. Who We Are

PQ-Aura is developed and maintained by Shreyas Sengupta, an independent developer based in India. When this policy refers to "we", "us", or "our", it means Shreyas Sengupta as the sole operator of the PQ-Aura website and services.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: peter_parker_2008@outlook.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate this service.

Data Type What We Collect Why
Payment Data Payment ID, order amount, currency (via Razorpay). We never see or store your full card number. To verify your purchase and deliver your license.
Contact Info Email address (optionally provided during checkout). To send payment receipts and license delivery.
Usage Logs HTTP request logs (IP address, timestamp, endpoint) on the Hugging Face Space server. Security monitoring and debugging. Retained for up to 30 days.
GitHub Pages GitHub may collect standard web analytics (IP, browser type) when you visit the website. Governed by GitHub's Privacy Policy.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels.
  • We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics service.
  • We do not collect any data from within your application that uses the PQ-Aura SDK. The SDK itself is fully offline and does not "phone home".
  • We do not store your full payment card details — these are handled exclusively by Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant).

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • License Fulfillment: Verifying your payment and delivering your commercial license key or agreement.
  • Customer Support: Responding to support requests, refund requests, or licensing questions you initiate.
  • Legal Compliance: Retaining transaction records as required under Indian tax and accounting law.
  • Service Security: Detecting and preventing abuse of the API server.

We will never use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to operate. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Razorpay — payment processing. Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting. Privacy Statement
  • Hugging Face Spaces — API server hosting. Privacy Policy

We choose these services carefully and share only the minimum information necessary for them to function.

6. Data Retention

  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian accounting regulations.
  • Server logs: Automatically purged after 30 days.
  • Support emails: Retained for the duration of the business relationship and up to 2 years after.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email peter_parker_2008@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We take security seriously — it's literally what this library is about. We implement the following measures:

  • All API communications are encrypted in transit via TLS/HTTPS.
  • Razorpay API keys are stored as encrypted secrets, not in source code.
  • The PQ-Aura server runs in an isolated container on Hugging Face infrastructure.

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

9. Children's Privacy

The PQ-Aura SDK and website are intended for developers and are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page. Material changes will be communicated to active commercial licensees via email.
